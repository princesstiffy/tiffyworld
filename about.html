Meet Tiffy

My name is Tiffany Rubio and I am 20 years old! I'm based in central Florida and do all of my work digitally. I usually work with Procreate and front-end development (HTML/CSS/JS).
So many of my interests are things I've loved since childhood, and I use those similar themes and designs in my own projects!

×

My Style

Nostalgia has always been such a dominant force in my life that has shaped who I am. A lot of my hobbies and interests today are nostalgic things, like old media and fashion that were popular when I was growing up. That girly, bright, and colorful 2000s style prominent in games and shows is a major inspiration for me today! Icons like Barbie and Polly Pocket raised me, and I try to achieve a similar theme of unabashed youthful femininity through my art!

×

My Inspo

I'm heavily inspired by dramatic and maximalist styles like Gyaru (specifically Himekaji and Agejo), McBling, Frutiger Aero, and Pink Paris Design. Lots of online and old web aesthetics that I loved growing up and continue to surround myself in!

Some of my artistic inspirations are:
Jeffrey Fulvimari, Park Suran, Takashi Murakami, Karen Duncan & Samantha Stringle (Bang on the Door), Polly Pocket, and MyScene!

×

Experience

Procreate: I have been using Procreate for years to produce digital illustrations and animations for branding and social media content.

Adobe Cloud: I use Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign to create digital designs like magazine pages, logos, and other artwork.

Web Development: I use Dreamweaver, Figma, and Visual Studio Code to create websites from scratch with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Extras: I have done some video production and animations (3D and 2D) using programs like Blender, Procreate, and Adobe Premiere Pro.
